About the Team

We are a competitive college soccer team with around 35 players, including student-athletes from Europe, Africa, North and South America. This season, we played 20 matches, winning 13, losing 5, and drawing 2. Despite a tough conference series, we won one match at the last second, showing our resilience and spirit.
